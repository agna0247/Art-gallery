Alleppey

📍 Location

Alappuzha District, Kerala

🌤 Best Time to Visit

November – February

🏞 Category

📝 Description

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is famous for its peaceful backwaters, houseboats and canals. It is one of Kerala's most popular tourist destinations.
